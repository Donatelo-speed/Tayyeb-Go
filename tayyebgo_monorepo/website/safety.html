Safety

Your safety is our priority

We've built TayyebGo with safety at its core — for customers, drivers, and partners alike.

Safety Features

Built-in protection

🚨

SOS Emergency Button

One-tap SOS button in the app instantly alerts our safety team and shares your location with emergency contacts.

📍

Real-Time Tracking

Share your live location with family and friends during any delivery. They can track your driver in real-time.

✅

Verified Drivers

Every driver goes through background checks, document verification, and vehicle inspection before going live.

📸

Photo Proof of Delivery

Drivers take a photo when delivering your order, ensuring accountability and resolving disputes quickly.

⭐

Rating System

Rate every delivery. Drivers below 4.5 stars are flagged for review. Your feedback keeps everyone safe.

🔒

Secure Payments

All transactions are encrypted. Never share cash with drivers — pay through the app for complete safety.
