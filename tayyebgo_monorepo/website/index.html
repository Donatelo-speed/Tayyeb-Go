FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is TayyebGo?

TayyebGo is a delivery platform that connects customers with local restaurants, stores, and services. We deliver food, groceries, pharmacy items, and anything you need.

How do I become a driver?

Download the TayyebGo Driver app, create an account, submit your documents, and start earning once approved.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash on delivery, Sham Cash, and credit/debit cards. More payment options coming soon.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 15-30 minutes. You can track your driver in real-time.

Can I schedule orders for later?

Yes! You can schedule orders for a specific date and time.

What is Anything Delivery?

Need something but can't go out? Request Anything Delivery and a driver will pick it up and deliver it to you — medicine, documents, gifts, anything.
