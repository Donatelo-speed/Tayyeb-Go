Orders

Order FAQs

Open the TayyebGo app and go to "My Orders". Tap on an active order to see real-time tracking with your driver's location on the map.

You can cancel within 2 minutes of placing your order. After that, the restaurant may have already started preparing it. Go to My Orders → tap the order → Cancel.

Check the tracking screen for updates. If your order is significantly late, contact support through the app or call us at +963 11 234 5678. We'll help resolve it.

If there's an issue with your order (wrong items, missing items, quality issues), go to My Orders → select order → Report Issue. Refunds are processed within 24-48 hours.

Account

Account FAQs

Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Phone Number. You'll need to verify the new number with an OTP code.

Go to Profile → My Addresses → Add New Address. You can save multiple addresses like Home, Work, and others.

Contact support at support@tayyebgo.com with your account email. Account deletion is processed within 7 days.

Payments

Payment FAQs

We accept Cash on Delivery, Sham Cash, and credit/debit cards. More payment options coming soon.

Your TayyebGo wallet holds your balance. Add funds via Sham Cash or card, then use it to pay for orders. You can withdraw funds anytime.

Go to Wallet → Add Funds. Choose your amount and payment method. Funds are added instantly.
